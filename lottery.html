LOTTERY
Instructions: Select 6 balls by clicking on them one at a time or simply hit 'random' to let the game choose for you. Click 'go' to play. You need to match at least 3 balls to get a score. Matching 3 wins 50 points, 4 wins 100 points, 5 wins 200 points and matching all 6 wins 500 points. Thank you for playing! This is a game by James Brain
